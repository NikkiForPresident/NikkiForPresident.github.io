Our Policies

As we campaign for the election, we have foreign and domestic policies that we hope will fit the wants and needs of the American people.

Trade with China

We plan to lift the current tariffs on China that were imposed by Trump and end the trade war. The current tariffs placed by president Donald Trump, do not serve a purrpose as other counteries are still able to trade with China. In addition, by lifting the tariffs on China we are able to reduce prices for American consumers as well as reduce the rising tensions between China and the United States.

Taiwan

Following the current U.S. policy for Taiwan we will not recognize Taiwan's sovereignty. Recognizing Taiwan's sovereignty will escalate the already high tensions between the U.S. and China and combined with the fact that Taiwan isn't strong enough to be independent could result in an outbreak of war between China and the U.S. for Taiwan.

North Korea

A hotly debated issue North Korea's possession of nuclear weapons is a serious problem. We believe diplomacy is still the best option. US threats have resulted in more aggression from North Korea such as more nuclear bomb and missile tests.

Latin America and Immigration

Although we do not support illegal immigration, we also do not support the way the federal government has been dealing with the issue of immigration. We support DACA for children who are already in the U.S., and do not support separating families or using tear gas at the border. The difference between legal and illegal immigration is that without screening we are likely to let in criminals. We believe that this screening process should be made more efficient.

Gun Control

We must reduce semiautomatic weapons and weapons with large-capacity magazines, the two types of weapons that are most dangerous in shootings. We also plan on requiring gun owners to securely store firearms in a locked container or with a tamper-resistant mechanical lock. This will keep guns out of the hands of kids who do not understand the gravity of what it means to own a gun. Lastly, we plan tostrengthen background checks on gun owners. Currently, under federal law only licensed gun dealers have to perform background checks.

Health Care

We plan to give access of free universal health care to all Americans, as it is an American right. According to the constitution, Americans are to be given “general welfare”, which supports health care. Additionally, half the world’s countries have universal health care and we should follow this policy for the better of the American people. This will provide the millions of Americans in need with important aid, as well as increasing productivity and helping the economy. This will also help fix the growing poverty of Americans.

Poverty

To address poverty we will increase the federal minimum wage to 10 dollars affecting 30% of the U.S. workforce. We will also provide subsidized employment creating new jobs for more people as well as rebuilding and devloping new infrastruture.

Climate Change

We strongly believe climate change is a serious issue that is occuring whether the current administration believes in it or not. To combat climate chang we will invest into new renewable energy, protect forests and reefs, and lay down new legislation to combat pollution.

Legalizing Marijuana

We believe in legalizing marijuana. By legalizing marijuana we will be able to generate 132 billion dollars in tax revenue for other services. In addition we would be able to eliminate the black market for marijuana consequently making it much safer to use.

About Us

Hello everyone, my name is Nikki Daniels and I am running for President of the United States with Alina Luckey as my Vice President. Our objective is to improve the nation’s future and bring in a perspective that advocates for the people. I empathize with the struggles of immigrants, as my mom immigrated to the United States from Iran at the tender age of 15 to flee the Iranian Revolution. She was forced to overcome innumerable obstacles just to get to America, a land of supposed peace and opportunity. My administration’s goal is to make sure all our citizens have their needs and concerns addressed and we hope to make this country a place they would be proud to call home. If we are elected, we will hold our stance and continue to ensure the safety and happiness of the nation’s citizens. However, for us to make progress, we need a starting point. If you agree with my platform, I ask you to stand with me in this presidential election. Let us work together to push this country forward. Pick Nikki, It’s Not Tricky!
